How It Works

Four steps. One browser tab. A physical object in your hand.

1

Write a Prompt

Describe what you want. "A bracket to hold my water bottle" or "A phone stand angled at 30 degrees." Plain English, no special syntax.

2

AI Generates Code

BuildFrog writes the CAD code — CadQuery, Build123d, or OpenSCAD. No conversion step, no mesh-from-image hack. Proper parametric geometry.

3

Preview & Tweak

See a live 3D preview in your browser. Adjust dimensions, change materials, iterate in seconds. Refine with follow-up prompts.

4

Slice & Print

Auto-slices for your Bambu Lab printer. Sends the job directly over LAN — no Bambu Studio, no Bambu Cloud, no extra steps.
